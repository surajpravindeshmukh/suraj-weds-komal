We are getting married

The dates are 22nd, 23rd & 24th of February 2024 and we would like you to be a part of it.

How we met

In life's beautiful mosaic, our journey began amid the cheerful setting of an office trip. Initial small talks grew into something, infusing each day of shared laughter and mutual understanding.

However, life's changes made me (Suraj) switch to a different company. During that time, we lost touch, our connection faded into the soft whispers of memory.

In 2022, amid the timeless charm of Hampi (Karnataka), our journeys unexpectedly intersected once again, a delightful coincidence that rekindled the warmth of our conversations.

In 2023, destiny led us to plan a trip to Bir (Himachal Pradesh) where realization dawned upon us. Bir, once just a destination, evolved into the heartfelt chapter where our intertwined stories unfolded the colors of possibility and newfound happiness.

Events

22nd February

Haldi 11AM - 11PM

At Suraj's home, the joyful Haldi ceremony will be celebrated (10AM - 2PM), adorning the groom-to-be with auspicious turmeric.

The vibrant Haldi ceremony will take place at Komal's residence (6PM - 10PM), where turmeric paste will be applied to bless the bride-to-be.

23rd February

Court Marriage 9AM - 6PM

Komal and Suraj will opt for a simple court marriage, exchanging vows and signing legal documents in the presence of witnesses, registering their commitment to solemnize their union.

Following the court marriage, Griha Pravesh ceremony for Komal will symbolize her traditional entry into her new home, marked by blessings, rituals, and warm welcomes by her new family members signifying the beginning of her new life.

24th February

Satyashodhak Marriage - Reception 6PM - 11PM

Komal and Suraj's wedding ceremony will reflect the ideals of equality, justice, and social reform cherished by Mahatma Jyotirao Phule and the Satyashodhak Samaj.

They will honor these principles by organizing a wedding that challenges traditional caste-based customs and rituals. Their ceremony will emphasize egalitarian values, striving to ensure social equality for everyone regardless of caste, creed, or social standing.

Your presence adds an extra layer of happiness to this special day, and the couple is truly excited to share these beautiful moments with everyone who's here to make it memorable.
